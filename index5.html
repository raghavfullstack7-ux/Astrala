Personal Year

Your Personal Year number reveals the theme, opportunities, and challenges you'll encounter in the current calendar year. It's calculated by adding your birth month and day to the current year, then reducing to a single digit (1-9) or master number (11, 22, 33).

This cyclical energy influences your experiences and helps you align with the universe's natural rhythms. Understanding your Personal Year allows you to navigate life transitions with greater awareness and purpose.

Numerology Compatibility Explained

Numerology compatibility goes beyond simple personality matching. It reveals how the core vibrational energies of two individuals interact, complement, and challenge each other.

Compatible numbers create harmony and mutual growth, while challenging combinations offer opportunities for learning and evolution.
